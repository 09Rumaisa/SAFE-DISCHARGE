SAFE-DISCHARGE AI
An Explainable AI Safety Copilot for High-Risk Hospital Discharge Decisions

1. Executive Summary
Healthcare professionals face intense cognitive and documentation overload, especially during hospital discharge, one of the highest-risk moments in patient care. Critical information such as medication interactions, follow-up requirements, and patient instructions are often missed, misunderstood, or poorly communicated‚Äîleading to preventable harm, readmissions, and clinician burnout.
SAFE-DISCHARGE AI is a clinician-facing AI Safety Copilot designed to reduce medical errors, improve patient understanding, and save clinician time during high-risk discharge workflows.
The system summarizes patient data, flags safety risks, generates explainable alerts with citations, and creates patient-friendly, multilingual discharge instructions, while learning continuously from clinician feedback.
The copilot is built using Microsoft Azure AI services, follows enterprise-grade security principles, and prioritizes ethical, inclusive, and explainable AI.

2. Problem Statement
Hospital discharge is a complex and error-prone process involving:
Multiple medications (often with interactions)
Pending lab results
Follow-up instructions
Patient education under time pressure
Key challenges:
Clinicians spend excessive time on documentation
Safety checks are manual and inconsistent
Patients often do not understand medical instructions
Language barriers worsen outcomes
Existing AI tools lack transparency and equity awareness

3. Proposed Solution
SAFE-DISCHARGE AI acts as a second pair of eyes for clinicians during discharge.
It does not replace doctors.
It supports safer decisions by:
Automating summaries
Flagging risks
Explaining why something matters
Ensuring patients understand their care
